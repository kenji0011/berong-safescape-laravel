Smoke Crawl Safety

Smoke rises to the ceiling during a fire, leaving clean air near the floor. Learn the crawl technique!

GET LOW & GO!

Always crawl under the smoke! Keep your head 12 to 24 inches above the floor where the air is cleanest and easiest to breathe.
