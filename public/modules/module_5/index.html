Protocol 1: Reactive

If your clothes catch fire, don't panic. These three moves are your ultimate defense. Master them now so you're ready for any emergency.

1

STOP

Stop right where you are. Do not run! Running gives the fire oxygen, making it grow bigger.

2

DROP

Drop to the ground and cover your face. Lying flat protects your face and lungs from the heat.

3

ROLL

Roll over and over until the fire is out. This smothers the flames and cuts off their air supply.
